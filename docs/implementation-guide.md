# VantaUM Implementation Guide

**Prepared for prospective TPA partners**

---

## Overview

VantaUM replaces the operational burden of utilization management with a fully managed service: concierge intake, AI-assisted clinical brief generation, and board-certified physician review — all under one platform with real-time visibility and SLA guarantees. Your team stays focused on member relationships while we handle the authorization workflow end to end.

---

## Implementation Timeline

| Phase | Weeks | Activities |
|-------|-------|------------|
| **Discovery & Planning** | 1–2 | Kick-off call, credentials exchange, network configuration, data format alignment |
| **Clinical Panel Alignment** | 3–4 | Specialty matching to your covered populations, state licensing verification, criteria calibration |
| **Intake Channel Setup** | 5–6 | eFax routing, email intake, API integration, provider portal provisioning |
| **Parallel Testing** | 7–8 | Live case shadow processing, SLA validation, workflow tuning, staff training |
| **Go-Live** | 9+ | Production cutover with full monitoring, daily check-ins transitioning to weekly |

Total time to production: approximately 8 weeks from signed agreement.

---

## What We Need From You

- **Member eligibility feed** — 834, CSV, or API; we adapt to your format
- **Benefit plan summaries** — so determinations reflect your specific plan designs
- **Clinical criteria preference** — InterQual, MCG, or your custom guidelines
- **Escalation contact** — a designated point person for edge cases
- **Fax routing** — forward your existing eFax number to us, or we provision a new one

That's it. No hardware, no software installs, no IT projects on your side.

---

## What You Get

- **Per-employee-per-month pricing** — no capital expenditure, no infrastructure to maintain
- **Concierge intake** — live, trained intake specialists (no phone trees, no IVR)
- **AI-powered clinical briefs** — structured summaries prepared before physician review
- **Same-specialty physician determinations** — matched to the authorization type
- **Real-time portal access** — providers and members can check status anytime
- **SLA-tracked turnaround** — 24 hours for urgent requests, 72 hours for standard
- **HIPAA-compliant audit trail** — every action logged and exportable
- **Monthly reporting** — auth volume, turnaround times, overturn rates, quality metrics
